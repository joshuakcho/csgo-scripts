====== Knife Slot Weapon Viewmodel Manipulation Script ======

- Keeps knife on left hand, while rest of weapon loadout (grendaes, rifles, etc) stay on the right
- Editable for right hand knife, left hand weapons, vice versa
- Incorporated static quickswitch, button x always pulls out knife, then back to primary/secondary
- All binds editable in script



====== To use: ======

- Put LeftHandKnife.cfg in ...\Steam\steamapps\common\Counter-Strike Global Offensive\csgo\cfg or take what you want from it and add to your autoexec config!
- Enter launch options at Steam > Library > Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (right-click) > Properties > Set Launch Options...
- Add "+exec LeftHandKnife.cfg" to launch options
