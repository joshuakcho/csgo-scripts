====== Map Practice and Grenade Trajectory Angles Script ======

- Kicks all bots, and resets autoteambalance
- Extends Roundtime and Roundtime_defuse to prevent constant round resets
- Incorporated sv_cheats for grenade trajectory lines and infinite money
- Able to buy weapons and grenades anywhere on map
- All commands editable in script



====== To use: ======

- Put prac.cfg in ...\Steam\steamapps\common\Counter-Strike Global Offensive\csgo\cfg or take what you want from it and add to your autoexec config!
- Load a map beforehand, by typing in console "map MAP_NAME"
- Enter in console "exec prac" when ready
- MUST have map loaded beforehand
